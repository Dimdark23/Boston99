Boston Public Garden

Why should you visit Boston Public Garden?

  1. The Boston Public Garden is a beautiful, historic park in downtown Boston's heart.
  2. It is one of the oldest public parks in the United States, dating back to 1837.
  3. The park is home to a stunning collection of plants and flowers, including over 80 species of trees, shrubs, and perennials.
  4. Visitors can take a leisurely stroll, picnic, or ride a swan boat on the park's tranquil lagoon.
  5. The Boston Public Garden is a must-visit attraction for anyone looking to experience the natural beauty and charm of Boston.
