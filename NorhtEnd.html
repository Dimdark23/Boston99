North End

Why should you visit Boston's North End?

  1. The North End is Boston's oldest residential community with a rich history.
  2. The neighborhood is famous for its delicious Italian cuisine.
  3. You can visit historic landmarks like the Paul Revere House and the Old North Church.
  4. The area has charming narrow streets and brick buildings that are perfect for exploring.
  5. You can enjoy stunning views of the harbor and access to several parks and public spaces.
